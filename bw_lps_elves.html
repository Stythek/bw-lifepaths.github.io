Elven Lifepaths

Legend

  • Containers represent (sub)settings.
    • Green Containers: Settings that contain a "Born" Lifepath.
    • Orange Containers: Subsettings without any "Born" Lifepath.
  • Boxes represent lifepaths.
    • Green Header: Lifepaths that can be chosen as a starting point during character creation.
  • Arrows indicate direct prerequisites.
  • Colors inside a lifepath are meant as a visual aid.
    • Green Title: Lifepaths that can be chosen as a starting point during character creation.
    • Yellow Box: The requirements for the lifepath (sometimes not represented with an arrow).
    • Yellow Box: Any specific restrictions.
